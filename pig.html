🐷 Pig

← Back to Mobs

Overview

Pigs are passive mobs that appear as pink quadrupeds with a distinctive snout. They are one of the most common animals in Minecraft and are valuable for food production. Pigs are peaceful creatures that never attack players and provide an important early-game food source through pork drops. They can also be saddled and ridden when given a saddle and direction with a carrot on a stick.

Health: 10 (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None (Passive)
First Appearance: Classic 0.24
Difficulty: Low

Behavior

Pigs are passive mobs that wander around aimlessly, eating the ground and occasionally emitting oinking sounds. They can be led using a lead or attracted with carrots or potatoes. Pigs can be saddled and controlled with a carrot on a stick to ride them as transportation. When struck by lightning, pigs transform into Zombie Pigmen (or Zombified Piglins in newer versions). Pigs are completely harmless and will not attack if provoked.

Drops

When killed, Pigs drop:

  • 0-3 Raw Pork (primary food source)
  • 0-3 Cooked Pork (if killed by fire or lava)
  • Saddle (if wearing one when killed)

Breeding and Transportation

Pigs can be bred by feeding two pigs carrots or potatoes, which will cause them to produce a baby piglet. Pigs can be saddled with a saddle item, allowing players to ride them. Riding a saddled pig while holding a carrot on a stick will make the pig move in the direction the player aims, providing an alternative form of transportation. Baby pigs take 20 minutes to mature into adults.
