💀 Wither

← Back to Mobs

Overview

The Wither is a boss mob and one of the most dangerous entities in Minecraft. It is a massive skeletal creature with three heads and a dark, ominous appearance. Unlike other mobs, the Wither is not found naturally in the world but must be summoned by the player. Defeating the Wither is a major achievement and provides access to the Nether Star, which can be used to craft Beacons.

Health: 300 (150 hearts)
Attack Damage: 8 + Wither effect
First Appearance: Release 1.4
Difficulty: Extreme

Behavior

The Wither is an aggressive boss that fires wither skulls at players and mobs. These projectiles explode on impact and inflict the Wither status effect, which damages players over time. The Wither is immune to most attack types and regenerates health if not dealt significant damage quickly. When damaged below half health, the Wither gains the ability to fly and becomes more aggressive. The Wither will attack any mob in sight, including other hostile mobs.

Drops and Rewards

When defeated, the Wither drops:

  • 1 Nether Star (used for crafting Beacons)
  • 50 Experience points

Summoning

To summon a Wither, players must place three Wither Skeleton Skulls in an "I" formation (stacked vertically on top of soul sand). This triggers the boss summoning sequence. The Wither will appear with its three heads and immediately become hostile. It's recommended to prepare with strong armor, weapons, and healing items before attempting this fight.
