Overview

Endermen are tall, dark, humanoid hostile mobs from the End dimension. They are unique among hostile mobs for their intelligence and teleportation abilities. Endermen are known for their distinctive appearance with elongated limbs and pale white eyes that glow menacingly. They are iconic to the late-game experience in Minecraft and are particularly dangerous in the End dimension.

Health: 20 (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 4 per hit (2 hearts)
First Appearance: Beta 1.8
Difficulty: High

Behavior

Endermen are neutral by default but will become hostile if a player looks directly at them (makes eye contact). They can pick up and place blocks from the environment, which can lead to landscape alterations. Endermen are capable of teleporting up to 32 blocks away to escape danger or pursue players. They take damage from water and rain, causing them to teleport away. Endermen are exceptionally fast runners and can deal significant damage quickly.

Drops

When killed, Endermen drop:

  • 0-1 Ender Pearl (used for crafting Eyes of Ender and teleporting)
  • Block in hand (if carrying one)

Special Notes

Endermen cannot be attacked by environmental damage except water and rain. They don't attack players unless provoked by eye contact. When killed in the End dimension, they drop more resources than those killed in the Overworld, making them valuable targets for late-game progression.
